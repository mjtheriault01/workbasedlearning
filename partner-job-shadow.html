Partnership Opportunity

Job Shadow

Observe. Explore. Connect.

At a Glance

A short-term observational experience — a few hours to 1–2 days.

Students observe your team's roles, workflows, and workplace culture firsthand. No supervision burden, no formal program—just a meaningful window into your world that helps students connect classroom learning to real careers.

Benefits for Your Business

  • ✓

    Showcase your team and career pathways

    Help students see what a future in your industry really looks like

  • ✓

    Identify students with early potential interest

    Start building a local talent pipeline from the ground up

  • ✓

    Build early awareness of your company

    Community presence that pays dividends in future hiring

Time Commitment

A few hours to 1–2 days

Fully flexible scheduling, set by you

Student Profile

  • →Early career explorers with curiosity
  • →Teacher-prepared with expectations set
  • →Ready to observe and ask great questions

Your Role

  • —Host students for an observation visit
  • —Allow brief Q&A with team members
  • —BHS staff coordinate all logistics

What You Should NOT Expect

✕

Not a working internship — students observe, they don't perform tasks

✕

Not a long-term commitment of any kind

✕

No prior student experience required — any student can participate
